About Us

The Reformed Church in Zambia (RCZ) Luanshya Town Centre is dedicated to spreading the word of God and serving our community. Founded with a vision to bring hope and spiritual growth to the people of Luanshya, we strive to create a welcoming environment for all believers.

Our mission is to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture faith through teaching and fellowship, and demonstrate God's love through compassionate service to our neighbors.
